# PostPunk Style Training Prep

This directory stores source material for future Taste Skill voice training.

The goal is to collect real examples and platform-specific notes before any automated voice pass is wired into PostPunk. These files are placeholders for curated evidence, not final brand law.

## How to Use This Directory

For each platform style guide:

- Add strong examples that already performed well or felt right.
- Add weak examples only when they teach a specific avoid rule.
- Preserve context, such as platform, date, campaign, target audience, and result.
- Note why an example works.
- Keep raw source copy separate from rewrite notes when possible.

## Files

- `facebook-style-guide.md` - community, personal, and product-aware Facebook voice.
- `linkedin-style-guide.md` - professional insight, business-aware, and founder/operator voice.
- `devto-style-guide.md` - technical article and developer-community voice.
- `pinterest-style-guide.md` - visual discovery, save intent, and pin packaging voice.

## Future Taste Skill Inputs

Future training should use:

- Approved posts that sound natural.
- Platform variants that prove PostPunk can adapt without generic cross-posting.
- Notes about hooks, sentence rhythm, CTAs, formatting, disclosure, and humor.
- Examples of drafts that were rejected for sounding too generic.

## Curation Rules

- Prefer real published posts over invented examples.
- Keep examples tied to a platform.
- Do not mix platform style rules into one generic voice file.
- Do not preserve phrases only because they were used once. Preserve patterns that repeatedly work.
- Record uncertainty instead of forcing a rule too early.
