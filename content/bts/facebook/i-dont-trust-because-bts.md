# I Don't Trust "Because" — Facebook BTS

Behind the scenes: I spent way longer on this than I expected.

What looked simple turned into debugging, second-guessing, rewriting things that "should have worked," and questioning whether I had somehow broken everything.

Eventually, one tiny change fixed it.

That's the weird part of building things. Most people only see the finished version. They don't see the tabs open at 1 a.m., the bad ideas that led to better ones, or the moments where you seriously consider setting the laptop on fire.

Progress rarely feels dramatic while it's happening. Sometimes it just looks like showing up again tomorrow with slightly better information than you had yesterday.

So here's my question:

What's one small win you've had lately?

It doesn't have to be impressive. Maybe you finished a project. Maybe you cleaned the kitchen. Maybe you finally made that phone call you've been putting off.

I'd genuinely love to hear it.
